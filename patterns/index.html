How to Read Patterns

Abbreviations and Symbols

  • CO | cast on
  • BO | bind off
  • SL | slip
  • YO | yarn over
  • tog | together
  • * | begin repeat
  • end | end repeat
  • [] X | repeat group X times

Examples

  • CO 18 sts. * K2 P2 end K2.
    cast on 18 stitches. Repeat four times: knit two purl two. Finally, knit two.
  • [YO, K2tog] 6
    repeat six times: yarn over then knit two together.
